Journal, Magazine, Newspaper Title Search

Type as much or as little of the journal, magazine, or newspaper title as you want. For example:

  • journal of counseling psychology
  • journal of counseling psy

This search engine will find magazines, journals and newspapers which the libraries own.

If your search is unsuccessful, try a TITLE or KEYWORD search.

To find individual periodical articles, use periodical indexes or databases such as EBSCO, Gail, etc.
